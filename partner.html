Why Partner With Us?

The Jamasi 1 Zongo Youth Sala Gala is more than a football tournament — it is a platform for youth development, community engagement, visibility, and local impact. We welcome organizations, brands, businesses, and supporters who want to be part of something meaningful.

Digital Partnership
